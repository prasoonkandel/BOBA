Bubble Tea

Bubble Tea is a sweet, creamy, and super fun drink with chewy tapioca pearls at the bottom. You sip it through a big straw, so you get both the drink and those little chewy bites in every sip—it’s like dessert and a drink rolled into one.

History of bubble tea

Bubble tea first popped up in Taiwan in the 1980s when someone had the genius idea to mix tea with milk, flavorings, and chewy tapioca pearls. It quickly became a hit, spread across Asia, and now it’s a global favorite with endless flavors and toppings.
